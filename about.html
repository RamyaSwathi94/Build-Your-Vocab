The software product described in this document is an interactive preparatory website called BUILD YOUR VOCAB. The purpose behind developing this product is to provide a platform where students who are aspiring for competitive exams like GRE, TOEFL, IELTS etc can learn and improve their vocabulary skills which is an integral part in such competitive exams. Students and even general public who are interested in improving their vocabulary skills can learn in a playful way through a hangman game.
